Dentistry

¿What is a career in dentistry??

Dentistry is the branch of medicine that deals with the study, diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of oral diseases. A dentist specializes in caring for teeth, gums, tongue, and gums.

¿Where you can study?

Some universities in Mexico where you can study dentistry:

  • UNAM (Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México)
  • UANL (Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León)
  • BUAP (Benemérita Universidad Autónoma de Puebla)
  • UAEH (Universidad Autónoma del Estado de Hidalgo)

¿Why study dentistry??

Because it allows you to help people, work in clinics or your own offices, and have a career with good job prospects.
