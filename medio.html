Environment

What is the environment?

The environment is everything around us: air, water, soil, plants, animals, and all living things. Taking care of it is essential for life on Earth.

Why is it important to protect it?

The environment gives us vital resources like oxygen, water, and food. It also regulates the climate, maintains biodiversity, and provides physical and mental well-being.

How can we help?

  • Reducing plastic use
  • Recycling and separating waste
  • Planting trees
  • Saving water and electricity
  • Walking or biking instead of driving
