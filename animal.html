Aquatic Animals

What are aquatic animals?

Aquatic animals are those that live in water, either in the ocean, rivers, or lakes. They can be fish, mammals, amphibians, and many other species that depend on water to survive.

Examples of aquatic animals

  • Dolphins
  • Whales
  • Sharks
  • Turtles
  • Jellyfish
  • Octopuses

Why should we protect them?

Because they are an important part of ecosystems and biodiversity. Pollution, overfishing, and climate change are serious threats to their survival.
